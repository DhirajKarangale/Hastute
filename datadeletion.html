Data Deletion Policy


Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data.


Unless the law specifies a different retention period, we will delete or anonymize your personal information as soon as it is no longer needed for the purposes for which we collected or used it in accordance with the preceding paragraphs. If Data must be kept for legal reasons, they are blocked and no longer available for further use.


You can request for data deletion


You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data.

To initiate a data deletion request, please send an email to helphastute@gmail.com.

We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe, as required by applicable laws and regulations.Data that might be deleted includes your account information, gameplay history, and preferences.

Data deletion requests will not impact any information shared with other players in online interactions.

We are not responsible for data that remains in backup copies or caches after deletion.

After your data is deleted, you may lose access to certain features and saved progress in our games.

If you have any questions about our Data Deletion Policy, please contact us at helphastute@gmail.com.
